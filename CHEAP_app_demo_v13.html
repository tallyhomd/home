Our Mission

Healthcare pricing in America is broken. The same MRI can cost $400 at one facility and $4,000 at another — and until now, patients had no way to know. CHEAP exists to fix that.

🏥 The Problem

Before the 2021 Hospital Price Transparency Rule, hospitals weren't required to publish their prices. Even today, data is buried in machine-readable files that consumers can't use. CHEAP makes that data human-readable and actionable.

💡 Our Solution

We aggregate CMS price transparency data from 6,200+ hospitals, normalize it, and surface it in a simple search interface — filtered by your insurance, your ZIP code, and ranked by total value (price + quality).

💰 How We Stay Free

CHEAP is 100% free for consumers. We earn revenue through fintech referral partnerships (Care Now, Pay Later), de-identified aggregate data licensing to researchers and insurers, and relevant healthcare advertising.

🔒 Your Privacy

We never sell your personal health data. Any data used for licensing is fully de-identified under HIPAA Safe Harbor standards. You control your data — always.

Care Now, Pay Later

Flexible ways to pay for care

CHEAP connects you to trusted patient financing partners so you can access necessary imaging, labs, and outpatient care now, and pay over time on terms that fit your budget.

How financing works

Instead of delaying care or putting unexpected bills on high-interest credit cards, you can apply for healthcare-specific financing at checkout. Many partners offer no-interest or low-interest plans, soft credit checks, and clear payoff timelines for qualified patients.

Featured partners

CHEAP is designed to plug into leading healthcare fintech options, including PayZen, Walnut, Cherry, PatientFi, Prosper Healthcare Lending, CareCredit, LendingClub Patient Solutions, Scratchpay, and Splitit. Specific partners may vary by market and provider.

Start a financing request

When you find a discounted cash offer that fits your needs, you can explore available financing directly from CHEAP. We surface partner options alongside your price quote so you can compare monthly payments before you book.
